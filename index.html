Mountains of Sharr

Kosovo

Batllava Lake

The Sharr Mountains form a mountain range in the Balkans that extends from Kosovo and the northwest of North Macedonia to northeastern Albania. The section in Kosovo is a national park, and the section in North Macedonia became a national park on 30 June 2021.

Batllava's Lake is located in Podujeva, a small town in the north of Kosova. In summer you can go there for swimming whereas in autumn people go there for fishing. It is a place with a lot of fresh air.
